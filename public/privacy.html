Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Applies to the Biome macOS application

1. Who we are

Biome is a desktop AI workspace application. For the purposes of UK data protection law, the data controller is the individual operator of the Biome service. You can contact us at openclaw.solution@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

When you request an access key through our signup form, we collect:

  • Your name — to personalise your experience
  • Your email address — to send you your access key and any important service updates
  • How you plan to use Biome (optional) — to understand our user base
  • The date and time of your request

We do not collect, store, or transmit any AI API keys you enter during setup. Those keys are stored only on your own device in local storage.

We do not collect the contents of your conversations with your AI agent. All conversation data stays on your device and passes between your device and the AI provider you configure (e.g. Google Gemini, OpenAI, or a local model).

3. How we use your data

  • To issue and validate your unique access key
  • To send you important service communications (e.g. changes to terms, security notices)
  • To understand who is using Biome and improve the product

Our lawful basis for processing is legitimate interests (providing and improving the service) and, where we send you email, contract performance.

4. How long we keep your data

We retain your name, email, and access key for as long as your account is active or until you ask us to delete it. If you request deletion, we will remove your record within 30 days.

5. Who we share data with

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your access key request data is stored on our backend server. When you use the app, your AI prompts are sent directly from your device to the AI provider you have configured (e.g. Google, OpenAI) — Biome does not proxy or log those requests.

6. Cookies and local storage

The Biome app uses your device's local storage (not browser cookies) to store your setup preferences and AI provider configuration. No tracking or advertising technologies are used.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at openclaw.solution@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Right to complain

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify active users by email.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: openclaw.solution@gmail.com
